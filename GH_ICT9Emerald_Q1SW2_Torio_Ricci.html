Characters from Noli Me Tangere

Visual Depictions Characters Descriptions
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
Mostly known as Crisostomo Ibarra, Ibarra is one of the main character in Noli Me Tangere. Some of the story is told in his perspective. He is an educated and well-respected young man who Padre Damaso is intimidated by.
Maria Clara
Maria Clara is Crisostomo Ibarra's love interest. In the story, she is described as very beautiful and alluring. However, Ibarra pays little attention to her during her first appearance in the story.
Padre Damaso
Padre Damaso is a Dominican priest. Even in the first few chapters, Padre Damaso is seen as the antagonist in the story. He is intimidated by Crisostomo Ibarra due to Ibarra being a wealthy and elite Filipino.
Padre Sibyla
Padre Sibyla is a Franciscan priest. Padre Sibyla, in the first few chapters, is mostly a neutral character. He frequently gets into arguments with Padre Damaso.
Donya Victorina
Donya Victorina is an old Filipino woman who acts as if she is from Europe. She is described negatively by the male characters as a woman who wears excessive makeup.
Don Rafael Ibarra
Don Rafael Ibarra was a powerful and wealthy man and was the father of Crisostomo Ibarra. He was arrested for allegedly murdering a soldier. However, this was not confirmed to be true since there was no solid proof that this happened. He then died in prison.
